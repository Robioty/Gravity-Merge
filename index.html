HOW TO PLAY

👆Drag to aim, release to drop a planet.
💥Two planets of the same type collide and merge into a bigger one.

⚠️Don't let planets pile past the danger line for too long — it's game over!
🌀Tap G to flip gravity — recharges slowly over time.
☄️A comet flies across the screen every so often — tap it to instantly recharge gravity!
✨Earn Stardust from merges. Spend it on NUKE and COLLAPSE power-ups.
